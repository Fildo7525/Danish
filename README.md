## Definite form of substantiv

**-ene**

    a chair -> the chair
    en stol -> stolen

    two charis -> the chairs
    to stole -> stolene

**-erne**

    a tomato -> the tomato
    en tomat -> tamaten

    two tomatos -> the tomatos
    to tomater - tomaterne

## Tenses

To say some time `ago` in danish

> __**NOTE:**__ for fire uge siden => 4 weeks ago
